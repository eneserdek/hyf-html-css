The Journey to Be a Web Designer

Blue Pill or Red Pill

Were you thinking to be a coder/
hacker/
programmer/
webdesigner and you lack in enough courage, freedom or information to do it?

Then you have come to the right place.
Why don't you check out which processes I have passed through?

But as a tradition I need to ask this question. Blue pill or red pill?

If you have chosen the red pill. Brace yourself!

  1. Since you have chosen the red pill, first thing first.

  2. Then, apply from clicking the picture below to create an account on the hackyourfuture.
    This will give a new direction to your future.

  3. Thirdly, create an account on freecodecamp.
    There, you will have chance to make your first self-development studies.
    These studies are mostly based on reading the instructions and doing small coding.
    So, it's really good for A1 level absolute beginners.

  4. Later, the most important part, I suggest you to buy a good headphone in order to listen this awesome radio and relax.
    For citizens, click here.
    For refugees, click here.

  5. After finishing the related coding courses on freecodeacademy, have an account on khanacademy.
    The courses in khanacademy is given with videos where the lecturer has a stunningly beautiful voice -just joking-.
    These videos help you very much after what you have theoretically learned at freecodecamp.

  6. Last but not least, thank me later by subscribing with your email address to my awesome website.
